PAREIDOPTCHA

Pareidolia is the tendency for the incorrect perception of a stimulus as an object, pattern, or meaning known to the observer. “Psychologists failed to realize that imagination is a necessary ingredient of perception itself.” -- Kant. We craft a web game to see the different perceptions between humans and AI.
