About Me

Hello! I’m Clement Liu. I am a Designer & Developer born in China, and currently based in San Francisco, California. I hold an MFA in Design from the California College of the Arts and have a BA Industrial Design from ECUST, China. I design for interactive web as well as for static graphics on and off the small screen. My work has been exhibited by California Academy of Sciences and CCA Hubbell Galleries, 20 Years of Form Studio Exhibition, and published by primer conference 2018. I've always been fascinated by human perception, and how we attempt to generalize understandings to the unknown. This curiosity led me to study the beauty of design and the experience they provided. I believe design curates moments for us to enjoy the experience itself.
