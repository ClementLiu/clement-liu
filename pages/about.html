About Me

My name is Clement Liu and I am a UX designer based in San Francisco, California.
I recently graduated with a Master of Fine Art in Design from the California College of the art and have a Bachelor degree in Industrial Design from ECUST, Shanghai, China. I've always been fascinated by human perception, and how we attempt to generalize and apply our own understandings to the unknown. This curiosity led me to study the beauty of design and the experience they provided. Design is curating the experience for users to enjoy the moment itself.
