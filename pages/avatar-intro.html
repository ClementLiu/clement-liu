Speculative and website design - Avatar

It’s a website developed for explaining FILTER project. It’s an interactive project with video and interactive device. People put on the helmet to become a physical world avatar. The video is about revealing the filtered world through by taking off the goggles to see the elephant in room.

See the site

Revealing banner To show the video’s idea that with the filter we can only see the parts of the reality and ignore the other side, I used a scrolling banner to compare the difference between filter and without filter.

Helmet I used interactive device to create the experience of filtering. I presented them at the nightlife of California Academy of Sciences to spark an interesting conversation between visitors about the online avatar and the filter effect in social behavior and explore the people’s attitude toward this phenomena.
