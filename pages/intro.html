Clement’s portfolio website

The goal was to present Clement's six-year design practice and design study, that contains his interest and passion in design through his 4-year industrial design at Shanghai, China, and 2-year MFA Design at California, US. Simple and clean, that would connect the related projects and interdiscipline skills.

Projects collection

Retrospected these years design experience, I enjoyed my rambling in and out design world. Facing all my previous works, I selected 21 projects showing my ethos. By showing them to friends and discussed, I got fresh views of these projects, which pushed me to iterate.

Prototype

After I draw the wireframes, I printed them out and ask others to do the paper test, which allows me to iterate on layout and functions instead of UI elements. Some early wireframes displayed projects details in index pages and use various interactions. When I showed it to my friends they seemed confused about where is the end of each project, so I decide to keep the first page simple and clear.
